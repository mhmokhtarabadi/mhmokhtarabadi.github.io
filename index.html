Mohamadhasan published on 2023-08-19
A simple two DoF inverse kinematics in MATLAB. 1. Introduction As you may already know, there are two major ways to solve Inverse Kinematics (IK) problems. Analytical, and Numerical approaches. In these two approaches, the solver will find a set of coordinates which specify the robot configuration in order to satisfy some constraints, for example position of a point or orientation of a link of the robot. However, in this blog we want to how a two DoF robot can travel a path to in order for it’s end point to reach a desired position.
