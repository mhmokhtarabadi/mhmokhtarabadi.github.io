About

I am a PhD student in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Minnesota. My research interests include:

  1. Robotics and Mechatronics
    • Rehabilitation and Assistive robotics
    • Human-Robot interaction and Haptic interface devices
  2. Computer Vision and Artificial Neural Networks
    • Markerless motion capture with applications in sports and physical therapy
    • Stereo vision and 3D reconstruction
  3. Classical and adaptive controller design for real-time applications
  4. Design, dynamic modelling, and prototyping electro-mechanical systems
